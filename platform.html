Android game

s

This is a 2D vertical platformer game developed in Android Studio as a University project. The player must navigate through levels as fast as possible whilst collecting coins and avoid enemies. Collecting coins, killing enemies and finishing the game without losing all contribute to the score. The player can save their username and score in an online database. Their score can then be accessed from the main menu. Here is a list of the features:

-Gravity Most of the entities are affected by gravity
-Goomba An enemy which moves left and right at intervals. The player can kill the goomba by jumping on the goomba's head. if the goomba hits the player from the side then the player loses
-Jumper An enemy which moves jumps left and right at intervals. Collision with the player works the same as the goomba
-Spikes Kills the player on contact
-Springs The player can jump on springs to jump higher than usual. This allows the player to reach the previous platforms
-Coins The player can collect these to gain points
-Leaderboard A highscore leaderboard stored on an online database using Firebase. At the end of the game, the player can enter their username to store their score on the leaderboard

The project repository can be found on my github here: https://github.com/MehdiSouameur/AndroidGame
