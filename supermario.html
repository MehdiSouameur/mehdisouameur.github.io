Pete's pizza party

This is a super mario knockoff game coded from scratch in C with an object-oriented structure. The player has to collect all the pizzas in the level in order to win. He can move right with D, move left with Q and jump with I. Here is a list of the different features and entities in the game:

-Spikes: On contact with spikes, the player will die and the game ends.
-Moving platforms: A platform which moves left and right or up and down.
-Floating pizzas: These pizzas are collected by the player on contact. Once every pizza in the level is collected, the game ends.
-Springs: When a player jumps on the top of a spring, he can jump higher.
-Falling bridge: When a player walks on this bridge, it will start to fall down.
-Enemies: Walks left and right and kills the player on contact. The player can kill the enemy by jumping on its head.

The project repository can be found on my github here: https://github.com/MehdiSouameur/Pizza-Party
