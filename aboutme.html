About me

Placement

For my placement year I was based in Reading. Urban Science is an international company specialising in providing business solutions to automotive companies. My role as an IT Operations Intern was mainly dealing with internal incident and service requests but also included aiding in the maintenance of the hybrid IT infrastructure of the company. As the company uses both on premise and Microsoft Azure, I was heavily exposed to cloud computing concepts particularly when trying to solve asset deployment issues.

During my placement I was sent to Frankfurt, Paris and Rome to manage and fix physical assets in the offices at these locations. I regularly worked with employees from around the globe, particularly Europe and the USA. I completed two certificates during this placement: ITIL V4 Foundation and Microsoft Azure Fundamentals.
